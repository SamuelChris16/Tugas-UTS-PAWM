Featured Project: Virtual ITB Campus Tour

Let's Take a Look at Our Project!

Virtual Campus Tour

Experience the ITB Ganesha campus in a fully immersive 3D environment. This project allows new students to explore facilities and landmarks from anywhere.

Historical Site Reconstruction

A digital preservation project to rebuild the Borobudur Temple in its prime. Users can interact with historical artifacts and learn about the site's history in VR.

VR Medical Training Simulation

A collaborative project with the Faculty of Medicine. This simulation provides a risk-free environment for medical students to practice complex surgical procedures.

AI-Driven NPC Interaction

This project integrates advanced language models (LLMs) to create non-playable characters (NPCs) that can hold dynamic, unscripted conversations.
